## Key User Pain Points

### Primary Challenges (Remote Teams)

**Task Allocation Issues:**
- Manual assignment wastes 2-3 hours/week per manager
- Skills mismatch leads to delays
- Workload imbalance causes burnout

**Deadline Management:**
- 67% of projects miss initial deadlines
- Poor visibility into realistic timelines
- External dependencies not tracked

**Communication Overhead:**
- Status update meetings consume 20% of work hours
- Async communication gaps
- Context switching between 6+ tools average

**Resource Visibility:**
- No real-time capacity tracking
- Can't predict bottlenecks before they occur
- Team utilization blind spots

[INSIGHT] Remote teams specifically struggle with: timezone coordination (not solved by current tools), async work handoffs, and "silent" blockers that go undetected.

## Strategy

The market is experiencing rapid adoption (doubled from $2.44B to $2.66B in one year) and is ripe for a specialized solution. Our immediate focus must be on capturing remote-first teams with our core AI differentiators before the market consolidates further.

### Phase 1: Go-to-Market (GTM) Strategy & 90-Day Roadmap

**Core Value Proposition:** The only AI PM tool that thinks like a remote team lead – auto-assigns based on skills + capacity, predicts realistic deadlines using your team's actual velocity.

**Target Beachhead:** 10-50 person remote-first companies, specifically in Tech/Software Development and Product/Design agencies.

**Monetization Model:** Freemium-led SaaS subscription model.

**Key Decisions for GTM:**
*   **Launch with a strong freemium offering to drive initial adoption and gather critical AI training data.**
*   **Prioritize content marketing and community engagement tailored to remote work challenges.**
*   **Secure 50 early adopter companies within 90 days by offering extended free access (6 months) in exchange for detailed feedback and case studies.**

**90-Day Roadmap:**

*   **Month 1: Foundation & Early Access**
    *   **Product:** Launch MVP with Smart Auto-Assignment (skill-matching), Deadline Prediction (team velocity), Timezone-aware Task Routing.
    *   **Marketing:** Develop core messaging & website. Initiate content strategy (blog posts, remote work guides) focused on pain points (task allocation, deadline misses for remote teams).
    *   **Sales/Growth:** Identify and onboard first 20 early adopter companies (6-month free offer) for product feedback and AI training data.
    *   **Partnerships:** Begin outreach to remote work communities/influencers.
*   **Month 2: User Engagement & Refinement**
    *   **Product:** Implement Async Stand-up Automation. Collect user feedback from early adopters; rapid iteration on core features.
    *   **Marketing:** Launch targeted LinkedIn/Twitter ads for "remote project management" keywords. Publish 2 case studies from early adopters.
    *   **Sales/Growth:** Onboard next 30 early adopter companies. Track conversion rates from initial free sign-ups to engaged users.
    *   **Data:** Establish AI learning loop with early adopter data to improve auto-assignment and prediction models.
*   **Month 3: Public Launch & Traction**
    *   **Product:** Refine onboarding flow based on user data. Prepare for public tiered pricing.
    *   **Marketing:** Announce public launch. PR outreach to tech/remote work publications. Host a webinar on "Mastering Remote Project Management with AI."
    *   **Sales/Growth:** Shift focus to converting free users to paid tiers. Develop outbound sales strategy for target beachhead.
    *   **Pricing:** **Formally launch paid tiers and offer the 6-month free offer as a limited-time incentive for new sign-ups during the launch period.**

**Pricing Tiers:**

| Plan       | Users              | Price/User/Month | Key Features                                                                                                                              | Target Segment               |
| :--------- | :----------------- | :--------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :--------------------------- |
| **Free**   | Up to 5 users      | $0               | Basic auto-assignment, manual task creation, basic deadline tracking, limited storage.                                                    | Startups, very small teams   |
| **Pro**    | Up to 25 users     | $9               | **Full AI auto-assignment (skills + capacity), predictive deadlines (team velocity), timezone-aware routing, async stand-up automation.** | Growing remote teams (10-25) |
| **Team**   | 25+ users          | $15              | All Pro features, plus advanced analytics, custom dashboards, 3rd party integrations (Slack, Teams), priority support.                    | Established remote teams     |
| **Enterprise** | Custom (50+ users) | Custom           | All Team features, plus SSO, dedicated account manager, custom integrations, SLAs, on-premise options.                                   | Large enterprises            |

[INSIGHT] The $7-12/user/month range is the sweet spot for SMB. Our Pro plan at $9 positions us competitively while clearly differentiating on core AI value. The 6-month free offer for early adopters is crucial for building our AI's intelligence.
